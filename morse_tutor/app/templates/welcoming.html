Where am I?

This free service is designed to teach Morse code, as well as to improve skills in receiving and transmitting signals. Training is suitable for people of different levels of skill, and even for those who have just decided to start.

How to start?

To start feeling Morse code, it is necessary to evoke associations in your head between a certain set of sounds (a sequence of dots and dashes) and the so-called "chants". To do this, you should use a mnemonic memorization technique.

If you press a key ~, you will see a table. Let's take an example: the letter A has an encoding Â·- (one short sound and then a long one). And it sounds like "ti - taaa".

To associate a letter with its sound, need to use an intermediate link - a chant. The chant for the letter A is a-GAIN. A short a, and then a long GAAAAIN.

So...

Turn up the sound and press the space bar. This key is used as a telegraph key. You can try to reproduce the sound of any character. Sing a chant at the same time. With time and a lot of practice, you will begin to associate morse sounds with this chant, and as a result, with the necessary symbol.
